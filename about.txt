## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is vinyl wrapping?
Vinyl wrapping is the process of applying premium adhesive vinyl film to surfaces like cabinets, worktops, doors, and furniture to transform their appearance without replacement.

### How long does vinyl wrapping last?
High-quality vinyl wrapping typically lasts 7-10 years or more with proper care and maintenance.

### Is vinyl wrapping cheaper than replacement?
Yes, vinyl wrapping is 60-80% cheaper than full replacement while achieving professional results.

### Can vinyl wrapping be removed?
Yes, professional vinyl wrapping can be removed without damaging the underlying surface.

### What surfaces can be wrapped?
Almost any smooth, clean surface including wood, MDF, laminate, metal, and some plastics.

### Do you offer warranties?
Yes, all our work comes with installation warranties, and the vinyl materials have manufacturer warranties.
