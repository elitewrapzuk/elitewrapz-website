Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a quote?

We aim to provide quotes within 24-48 hours of receiving your enquiry. For complex projects, we may arrange a site visit first.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer free, no-obligation consultations and site visits throughout Birmingham & The Midlands.

What information do you need for a quote?

We'll need to know what you want to wrap, approximate dimensions, your location, and any specific finish preferences. Photos are helpful but not essential.

How quickly can you start my project?

Lead times vary depending on our schedule and project complexity. Typically, we can start within 1-2 weeks of quote approval.

Do you work weekends or evenings?

Yes, we offer flexible scheduling including evening and weekend installations for both residential and commercial clients.

What's your coverage area?

We cover Birmingham and The Midlands. Contact us for projects outside this area - we may still be able to help.
