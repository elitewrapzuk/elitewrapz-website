Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Elite Wrapz vinyl wrapping services

How much does kitchen vinyl wrapping cost compared to a new kitchen?

Kitchen vinyl wrapping typically costs £450-£2,500 depending on the size of your kitchen and vinyl brand chosen (Coverstyl, LX Hausys, or 3M Di-Noc). This is 70-80% cheaper than a new kitchen, which costs £10,000 or more.

Elite Wrapz serves Birmingham and the Midlands, offering three premium vinyl ranges to suit different budgets while delivering the same transformation at a fraction of the cost.

How long does vinyl wrapping last on kitchens?

Our premium vinyl films are designed to last 10-15 years with proper care. All three brands we use (3M Di-Noc, Coverstyl, and LX Hausys) are specifically engineered for high-use environments.

The vinyl is heat and water-resistant, won't peel or bubble when installed correctly, and maintains its finish through daily use. We provide care instructions to help you get the maximum lifespan from your wrap.

Can you wrap any style of kitchen?

Yes! We can wrap virtually any kitchen style - from modern flat-panel doors to traditional shaker styles, curved edges, and even awkward corners. The vinyl is incredibly flexible and can be applied to:

  • Kitchen cupboards and drawers
  • Worktops and islands
  • Appliance panels
  • Kickboards and plinths
  • Walls and splashbacks

As long as the surface is smooth and properly prepared, we can wrap it. We offer over 2,500 finishes including wood grains, marble effects, solid colours, and metallic finishes.

What's the installation process and how long does it take?

Most projects are completed in 1-3 days depending on the size and complexity. Here's our process:

  1. Free Consultation: We visit your property, assess the project, show you samples, and provide an accurate quote
  2. Surface Preparation: We clean and prime all surfaces to ensure perfect adhesion
  3. Professional Installation: Our trained installers apply the vinyl using specialist heat tools and techniques
  4. Quality Check: We inspect every detail to ensure a flawless finish
  5. Handover: We provide care instructions and answer any questions

You can use your kitchen the same day - there's no drying time or fumes!
