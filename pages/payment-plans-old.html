PAYMENT PLANS

Flexible payment options to suit your budget - spread the cost with 0% interest or our in-house plan.

Option 1: PayItMonthly

0% Interest Finance Available

We've partnered with PayItMonthly to offer you flexible payment options with 0% interest on selected terms.

Key Features:

  • 0% APR available on 2-12 month terms
  • Quick online application process
  • Instant decision in most cases
  • Available for projects £500 and above
  • No hidden fees on 0% terms
  • Spread the cost over 2-12 months

How It Works:

  1. Get your free quote from Elite Wrapz
  2. Apply for finance through PayItMonthly
  3. Receive instant decision
  4. Schedule your installation
  5. Pay in easy monthly installments

Representative Example (0% APR):

For a purchase of £2,000 over 10 months:

  • 10 monthly payments of £200
  • Total amount payable: £2,000
  • Interest charged: £0
  • APR: 0%

Option 2: Wrap-n-Relax In-House Plan

Flexible Payment Terms Directly With Us

Our Wrap-n-Relax payment plan offers extended terms for larger projects, managed directly by Elite Wrapz.

Key Features:

  • Payment terms from 3-24 months
  • Simple approval process
  • No credit checks required
  • Minimum project value £1,000
  • Direct relationship with Elite Wrapz
  • Flexible payment dates

Payment Terms:

  • 30% deposit required
  • Remaining balance spread over agreed term
  • Monthly payments via Direct Debit or Standing Order
  • Early settlement options available

Example (12-month term):

For a project valued at £3,000:

  • Deposit (30%): £900
  • Remaining balance: £2,100
  • 12 monthly payments: £175
  • Total payable: £3,000 + interest charges

Interest rates vary based on project value and term length. Contact us for a personalized quote.

Which Plan Is Right For You?

PayItMonthly

Best for: Smaller projects (£500-£3,000) wanting 0% interest

Terms: 2-12 months

Approval: Credit check required

Wrap-n-Relax

Best for: Larger projects (£1,000+) needing longer terms

Terms: 3-24 months

Approval: No credit checks

Important Information

PayItMonthly Terms:

  • Subject to status and affordability checks
  • 0% APR is available on selected terms (typically 2-12 months)
  • Alternative terms with interest may be offered if 0% APR is not available
  • You must be 18 or over and a UK resident
  • Full terms and conditions provided during application
  • PayItMonthly is a trading style of Pay4Later Limited, FCA registered
  • Elite Wrapz acts as a credit broker and not a lender

Wrap-n-Relax Plan Terms:

  • 30% deposit required before work commences
  • Remaining balance must be paid in equal monthly installments
  • Interest charges apply (rates provided upon application)
  • Payment terms are agreed in writing before work begins
  • Missed payments may result in late fees and project delays
  • Elite Wrapz retains ownership until full payment is received
  • Early settlement discounts may be available
  • Subject to Elite Wrapz approval

General Terms:

  • All finance applications are subject to approval
  • Finance availability may vary based on project type and value
  • Final quotes include all material and labour costs
  • Payment plans cannot be combined with other offers unless stated
  • Prices quoted are valid for 30 days from quotation date
